--- title: Mode Survey of local transportation description: Providing actionable real-time data to businesses and policy makers ---

How do people really reach local stores?

Real-time data from anonymous, in-person surveys.

The Mode Survey box allows consumers to indicate which mode of travel they take to reach local small businesses, sharing results online anonymously and wirelessly.

Why participate in the survey?

  • Sharing your mode of travel helps us respond to your needs more precisely
  • Easy way to survey consumers in a way that respects their privacy
  • Encourages consumers to consider other modes of travel on congested streets
